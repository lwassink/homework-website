Vegan Mac and Cheese

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Description

There are many recipes for vegan mac and cheese. Most are gross. Some even use daiya cheese. This one is shockingly delicious.

Ingredients
  • 10 oz dried macaroni
  • 8 oz yellow potatoes
  • 1 medium carrot, diced
  • 1 small yellow onion, diced (about 1/3 cup)
  • 1/2 cup raw cashews
  • 1/4 cup Crisco
  • 2 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • about 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper

Directions
  1. Cook macaroni to al dente, drain, and set aside.
  2. Bring several cups of water to a boil. Add potatoes, carrot, and onion. Cook until tender, about 10 minutes.
  3. Transfer cooked veggies to a blender. Add 3/4 of their cooking liquid, along with all other ingredients. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour over noodles. Add salt to taste Serve immediately.
